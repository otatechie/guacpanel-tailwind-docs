# Final Lighter Retro Color Scheme

## Light Mode:
- **Primary Color**: `#3a3a3a` (Lighter Charcoal Gray)
  - Even softer than previous #2a2a2a
  - ~23% lighter than pure black (#000000)
  - Easier on the eyes
  - Still maintains good contrast

## Dark Mode:
- **Background**: `#252525` (Lighter Dark Gray)
  - Softer than previous #1a1a1a
  - ~15% lighter than pure black
  - More comfortable for extended viewing
  
- **Text/Borders**: `#d4d4d4` (Light Gray)
  - Softer than pure white (#ffffff)
  - Better for dark backgrounds
  - Reduces glare

## Color Progression:
```
Pure Black: #000000 (too harsh)
Previous: #2a2a2a (good)
Current: #3a3a3a ✓ (even better - lighter)

Pure Black BG: #000000 (too harsh)
Previous: #1a1a1a (good)
Current: #252525 ✓ (even better - lighter)
```

## Benefits:
✅ Even softer retro minimal aesthetic
✅ Further reduced eye strain
✅ Better readability for long sessions
✅ More refined and modern look
✅ Still maintains high contrast
✅ Comfortable in both light and dark modes
